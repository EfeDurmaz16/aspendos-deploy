YULA Logo — Concept Comparison

Three letter-free symbolic marks. Each shown mono (light + dark), accent-color (light + dark), as wordmark pairing, and at 5 sizes down to favicon (16px).

01 · Reversibility Arc

Two arcs seal a loop around a signature dot. Reads as: every action is signed, and the action can be undone. The gap in each arc is the "rewind affordance". Matches the product's core promise (prove-what-they-did + reversible) without being illustrative.

02 · Signet Seal

Outer ring + three radial marks at 90°/210°/330° + center dot. The three marks represent the trust triad: FIDES · AGIT · Reversibility. Reads as a stamped signet — the thing that makes a letter provable, which is exactly what YULA does for every tool call.

03 · Keystone

The wedge at the apex of an arch — the single stone that locks everything in place. Historical rhyme: the original project name (Aspendos) is the city whose Roman theater's keystone is legendarily perfect. Semantically: trust is the keystone of the agent stack. Bold geometric trapezoid.
